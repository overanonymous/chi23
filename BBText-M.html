The % Daily Value(%DV) shows how much a serving contributes to each nutrient. The %DV column doesn’t add up to 100%. Instead, the %DV is the percentage of the Daily Value for each nutrient in a product’s serving. Use %DV to determine if a product’s serving is high or low in an individual nutrient, where 5% DV or less of a nutrient per serving is considered low. However, 20% DV or more of a nutrient per serving is considered high. You can use the %DV to compare foods and choose products higher in nutrients you want to get more of and lower in nutrients you want to get less of in your daily diet.
